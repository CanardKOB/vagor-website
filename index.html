Unique Atmosphere

Every single one of our hotels is unique in the sense that it aims to bring the magic that comes with traveling and discovering new places right into your overnight experiences. Each one of our seven locations was designed with care and thought so it immerses you into the culture of the surrounding area. You’ll find that everything from our signature restaurant menu, to the curtains in your room has been handpicked for your enjoyment.

Attentive Concierge

Our staff strives to create lasting memories for guests. Sincere recommendations, laundry service, restaurant reservations, spa appointments, and more can be easily arranged with our dedicated and professional concierge team who aim to give you a memorable experience. The lounge is open 8:00am to 11:00pm for all guests and after hours, guests are welcome to use the concierge station’s iPad, which is loaded with local connections to find the best activities, restaurants, and more.

Complimentary Parking

This may not seem like much, but when you’re a busy traveler making the most of your on-the- go vacation it’s nice to have reliable transportation. That’s why when you stay at any Vagor Hotel, you’ll benefit from free-self parking and, if needed, a free shuttle transport to the areas most popular attractions. We don’t have valets or an automated parking garage - meaning you and your party may come and go as you please.

Explore what the world has to offer with VAGOR hotels!

The crowning jewel of Vagor hotels are our unique excursion packages. From conquering the highest peak in the Northeastern United States in one day to enjoying a 4-day historic tour of the Loire Valley - you can do it all!

Our excursion packages come in a wide range of prices, times, and activities so everyone can enjoy them. At the core of Vagor hotels is the belief that the world should be explored. So, when you book one of our packages at reservation or with our concierge you’re guaranteeing yourself the experience of a lifetime.

If you want a more in-depth look at the packages we offer, you can click the button down below, which sorts the experiences by location. Then, you can either select your excursion package during your reservation or book it with the hotel’s concierge by phone or at the concierge desk in the designated lounge of your hotel. We are constantly updating our list of excursions so you can be assured that you’re getting all the correct information, making your trip relaxing and hassle-free.

Disclaimer: While we recommend booking your Vagor excursion when you reserve your room, we cannot ensure availability for anyone. We rely on a first-come, first-serve system so make sure you book your preferred excursion ASAP because spots do sell out fast.
